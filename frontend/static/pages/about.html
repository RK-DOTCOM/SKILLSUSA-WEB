About Metea Valley SkillsUSA

Empowering students with leadership skills, technical expertise, and opportunities to thrive in career education.

What is SkillsUSA?

SkillsUSA is the #1 workforce development organization for students, helping students become a skilled workforce, career-ready leaders, and responsible citizens.

The current membership of over 413,000 students and teachers brings the SkillsUSA Framework of Personal Skills, Workplace Skills, and Technical Skills Grounded in Academics into education programs nationwide.

Through various competitions, events, and real-life exposures, SkillsUSA prepares its members with the most in-demand careers and helps to bridge the skill gap in leadership, confidence, and career readiness.

Since the creation in 1965, it has guided innumerable people into professional and personal success.

Our Mission

The mission of Metea Valley SkillsUSA is to prepare students for careers in the skilled workforce by developing their technical and leadership skills through hands-on competitions, leadership activities, and community service.

We strive to inspire excellence in students and foster a passion for lifelong learning and career success.

Our Values

Leadership

Through your various experiences with Metea SkillsUSA, we hope that you will inspire others by leading by example along with demonstrating your vision, purpose, and positive influence.

Throughout this, you will develop strong communication and collaboration skills allowing you to move forward with confidence. Over time this will develop your leadership qualities where you will empower not just yourself, but others around you!

Responsibility

Responsibility is our commitment to excellence where all members are responsible for maintaining high standards in their work and behavior.

Responsibility plays a critical part in success as members are required to complete tasks, meet deadlines, and work towards overall growth.

But with these principles members align with SkillsUSA's standards including service to the community and progress.

Our Impact

We pave the way to making our members' future successes. Instilling career readiness in technical skills also includes various soft skills such as communication and collaboration.
