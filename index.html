What is SkillsUSA?

SkillsUSA is a national organization that empowers students to excel in their careers and become leaders in their communities. The Metea Valley SkillsUSA Chapter helps prepare students with the essential skills needed for success in the workforce and beyond.

Our chapter offers numerous opportunities for growth, leadership development, and technical excellence in fields ranging from engineering to business and information technology.
