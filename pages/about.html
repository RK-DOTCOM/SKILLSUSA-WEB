About Metea Valley SkillsUSA

Empowering students with leadership skills, technical expertise, and opportunities to thrive in career education.

What is SkillsUSA?

SkillsUSA is a national nonprofit organization that helps students develop the skills they need to excel in their careers. We focus on empowering individuals in areas such as leadership, technical skills, and workforce development. The organization provides opportunities for students to showcase their skills in regional, state, and national competitions.

Our Mission

The mission of Metea Valley SkillsUSA is to prepare students for careers in the skilled workforce by developing their technical and leadership skills through hands-on competitions, leadership activities, and community service. We strive to inspire excellence in students and foster a passion for lifelong learning and career success.

Our Values

Leadership & Excellence

We believe in empowering students to be leaders in their fields. Our programs foster critical thinking, creativity, and problem-solving, equipping students for success in their careers and personal lives.

Integrity & Innovation

Our values are built on integrity, where we encourage innovation and excellence, creating an environment where students can grow, learn, and contribute meaningfully to society.
