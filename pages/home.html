About Metea Valley SkillsUSA

SkillsUSA at Metea Valley is a student-led organization that provides opportunities for high school students to excel in career and technical education. Our chapter emphasizes leadership, teamwork, and skill-building in various fields such as engineering, healthcare, business, and more!

Competitions & Events

Compete & Showcase Your Skills

We participate in numerous local and regional competitions, where our members demonstrate their talents in various technical and leadership fields. Whether it’s in welding, business management, or technology, there’s an event for everyone!

At Metea Valley SkillsUSA, we prioritize hands-on learning and real-world applications. Our students have the opportunity to master the latest technologies and tools in their respective fields, ensuring they’re prepared for the workforce.
